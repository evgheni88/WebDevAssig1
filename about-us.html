About us

WE LVE TO BUILD HOMES

Novus Construction Ltd was founded with one mission in mind: to restore the old and build the future of home interior and exterior. We want to help people design and create the inside of their home to reflect and express the uniqueness of the people living in the house.

We also design and create the outside of the house, making sure the client's unique vision is immediately visible.

For six years we've been designing, creating, and building the most beautiful kitchens on the Irish market, and thanks to our many satisfied clients we continue to grow as a company.

Our story began when we realised people wanted not just functional but an expression of self in the design. We decided to listen, and we have continued to listen, and our clients have taught us to always make sure it's their design throughout the process. The client is always in focus when we design, create and build.

We help to bring the client's vision to life. We only build what works for our customers, and it's always unique and functional.

Our work is art, but we can't let you into all of our customer's home. Thankfully we have gotten permission from enough clients to showcase our attention to detail and perfection in everything we do. Have a browse and be inspired.

As Novus Construction continues to restore the past while incorporating the future, we hope to continue to expand as our clients share their experience working with us, adding Novus Construction in a storyline when showcasing their new home, helping us grow.

Novus Construction, based in Ireland, offers expert construction services with a focus on quality and client satisfaction. Our skilled team ensures every project is a testament to our commitment to excellence.
